An initiative to build model schools by using integrated manner of innovative teaching-learning methods, that will become a benchmark of excellence for educating underprivileged children.

About Learn Labs

Mission to create an innovative model of education that is measurable, sustainable and replicable, so as to provide quality education to all mainstream schools.

Objective

To develop a portfolio of solutions which will tackle theissues of inequality in education and low quality of teaching methods in our education system. We will do this by creating a Model schools which will become examples of high quality public schools and also labs from where we will generate a portfolio of resources and services to make a positive impact the education system.

Current Progress

We belong to a community of people who have:

- Written books on education, children and learning;
- Devoted their lives to the world of children;
- Successfully started alternate schools around the world;
- Introduced ‘unschooling’ of archaic methods;
- Promoted homeschooling;
- Written blogs and articles on educating children on the web;
- Shared TED talks and other Youtube videos;
- Met us and our children and shared their journey,


This is a group of dedicated facilitators and parents who are ready to explore the other methods of imparting and sharing education. However, the biggest architects of learn labs are children themselves, who have been questioning, exploring all the time and contributing to the learning methods with imagination.

The community we serve is the backbone of our project. You and your children are the inspiration behind our efforts. We are also grateful to our supporters and wellwishers. Without your support, we would not be where we are today.
